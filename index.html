The problem

Goals aren’t meant to be a solo sport.

Staying consistent is hard when you feel alone. Goalden brings your people into the process so plans don’t fade and goals don’t get forgotten.

“I start strong, then fall off.”

People struggle to stay consistent with goals when they feel alone, and motivation fades fast without accountability.

How Goalden helps: Social goal‑setting with shared progress, gentle nudges, and a feed that celebrates every win.

“Our plans always fall apart.”

Group plans fall apart because no one organizes, no one follows through, and details get lost in random chats.

How Goalden helps: Built‑in event planning tools, reminders, and clear ownership so your gatherings actually happen.

“Social media doesn’t make me better.”

Social media shows achievements, but it doesn’t help people actually achieve them or feel truly supported.

How Goalden helps: A motivation feed designed for progress, not perfection—where encouragement is the default.

“We keep saying we’ll get together…”

Families and friends want to connect more, but life gets in the way and reunions never leave the group chat.

How Goalden helps: Shared goals, coordinated schedules, and challenges that bring everyone back together in real life.
